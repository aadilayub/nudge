What's new in Nudge

1. Add your daily goal

The slider page now asks the simple question 'What do you want to do today?'.

Click on it and you'll be able to put in your daily goal, which will reset at midnight.

This is inspired by your suggestions that Nudge should help nudge you towards good stuff, not just away from bad stuff.

3. YouTube Autoplay Stopper
