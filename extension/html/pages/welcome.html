Welcome to Nudge

My name's Louis and I'm the founder of Nudge — I hope it makes your life a little bit better.

I take your privacy extremely seriously. Nudge tracks anonymised data, which I need to make it work better. I can't see any specific URLs or content that you look at, any of your activity other than on Nudge sites, or anything that identifies you to me. But I can see time spent on Nudge sites, and usage of Nudge features.

P.S. Feel free to share Nudge (nudgeware.io) or give me your feedback on feedback@nudgeware.io
